About Me

I grew up in Masvingo, Zimbabwe and moved to Massachusetts in 2020 to study Computer Science and Statistics at Amherst College. I enjoy all things data - scraping, wrangling and analysis - using languages such as R, Python and SQL and tools such as Tableau and OpenRefine. On the side, I enjoy reading, write and do photography.

Projects

Below is a selected list of projects that I have worked on. Click to learn more

Pepcity

Analyzing Pep Guardiola's impact on Manchester City's performance using tweets scraped from Twitter and a dataset obtained from Kaggle.

Decoding Diversity

A Neural Network to determine the age, gender and ethnicity of users based on their images using image data obtained from Kaggle.

tweetGPT

An analysis of what twitter was saying about ChatGPT between December 2022 and April 2023 using tweets scraped from Twitter.

Zhara

An analysis of food security in Zimbabwe using data from the Food and Agriculture Organisation, the Zimbabwean National Statistics Agency and Ministry of Agriculture and Land.

Pigflation

An analysis of US Tech layoffs investigating the economic impact of recent Tech layoffs, their national and state distribution and the characteristics of the companies involved.

AI bot

A tangible AI tool that uses the GPT3-model, Optical Character Recognition and an HTML-CSS-JavaScript web app to read handwritten notes and generate responses in the text class.
